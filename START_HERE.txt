WHAT THE ALGORITHM DOES:

1. Detects dominant market cycles (18-month through 2.5-week)
   - Uses FFT spectral analysis
   - Finds 6 Hurst nominal cycle periods
   - Scores confidence for each cycle

2. Builds curvilinear envelopes (support/resistance zones)
   - Linear interpolation (standard)
   - Parabolic interpolation (Appendix 5, smoother)
   - Uses for turning points and stops

3. Computes Hurst's moving averages
   - Half-span (primary timing signal)
   - Full-span (cycle removal)
   - Inverse (cycle magnitude extraction)

4. Generates buy/sell signals
   - Edge-band: earliest entry (price crosses envelope)
   - Mid-band: confirmed entry (price crosses half-span MA)
   - Confluence scoring: multiple cycles aligned = stronger signal

5. Backtests with risk management
   - Envelope-based stops
   - Position sizing by risk %
   - Full metrics: Sharpe, win rate, drawdown, R-multiples

6. Validates with walk-forward testing
   - Train/test splits across time
   - Out-of-sample performance verification
   - Prevents overfitting

7. Analyzes with ablation testing
   - Disable each cycle, measure impact
   - Identify which cycles matter
   - Confirm theory matches practice

DATA REQUIREMENTS:

- Daily OHLC (Open, High, Low, Close)
- Minimum: 300 bars (2+ years of daily data)
- Recommended: 1000+ bars for robust cycle detection
- Sources: CSV file, Yahoo Finance (yfinance), Quandl, or synthetic data

Supported markets: Any liquid instrument
- Crypto (BTC, ETH, etc.)
- Forex (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, etc.)
- Stocks (individual, indices like SPY)
- Commodities (gold, oil, etc.)

================================================================================

CONFIGURATION:

Risk per trade: 1-5% of capital (default 2%)
Confluence threshold: 0.2-0.5 (higher = fewer, stronger signals)
Edge-band signals: Buy at earliest opportunity (less confirmation)
Mid-band signals: Buy after confirmation (more reliable)
Walk-forward windows: Test on unseen data (OOS validation)
Ablation analysis: Measure importance of each cycle component

See EXAMPLES.md for custom configuration examples.

KEY INSIGHTS:

3. Confluence of cycles predicts turning points
